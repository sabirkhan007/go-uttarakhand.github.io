Brief Description

One of the finest trekking experiences for you would be hiking in the Garhwal region and discovering the isolated valleys of Uttarakhand. The Rupin Supin Trek is as entertaining as the name suggests. The Valleys, which are located in Uttarakhand's northwestern region and on the border with Himachal Pradesh, are among the most gorgeous and lovely in the state. Trekking along the Rupin River inside the Govind Pashu National Park's borders will be a life-changing event for you. You will get the opportunity to explore the two valleys in a beautiful trip through high hills, viewing the massive Himalayan peaks, primarily the Kinnaur Kailash Range, in the Garhwal Himalayas. The trip to Rupin Supin will wow you with its bizarre vistas, snow-capped mountains, and isolated valleys of Garhwal and Kinnaur at every stride and turn.

The Rupin Supin Trek begins in Uttarakhand, in Bheetri. From there, we'll go to Kurla Thaach, where we'll pass through a few woods before arriving to Rikatik Odi. Take a few seconds to appreciate the magnificent views of the valley from this location, which connects the Rupin and Supin Valleys. To go to the Supin Valley, you'll have to pass through a few more Himalayan towns, leaving the Rupin Valley behind. Enjoy the comforts of homestays before returning to Dehradun and spending the night at Sankri.

Itinerary

Short Itinerary

  • Day 1: From Dehradun to Himri
  • Day 2: From Himri to Bheetri to Kurala Thaach (8 km)
  • Day 3: From Kurala Thaach to Rikatik Odi (8 km)
  • Day 4: From Rikatik Odi to Phitadi Odi (8 km)
  • Day 5: From Phitadi to Jakhol
  • Day 6: From Sankri to Dehradun

Begin your journey from Dehradun to Himri, a scenic drive offering views of lush landscapes and mountainous terrain. Upon arrival at Himri, settle into your accommodations and prepare for the trekking days ahead. Explore the surroundings and enjoy a restful night.

Start early from Himri, trekking through Bheetri village and reaching Kurala Thaach. This 8 km trek offers stunning views of the valley and forests. Enjoy the peaceful surroundings and settle into your camp for an overnight stay under the stars.

Continue your trek from Kurala Thaach to Rikatik Odi, covering 8 km of picturesque trails. Along the way, marvel at the natural beauty, dense forests, and alpine meadows. Reach Rikatik Odi and enjoy a warm meal before retiring for the night.

Trek from Rikatik Odi to Phitadi Odi, an 8 km journey through varying terrains. This trail offers glimpses of local wildlife and stunning vistas. Upon arrival, rest and prepare for the next leg of the journey while enjoying the serene surroundings.

Descend from Phitadi to Jakhol, passing through beautiful valleys and charming villages. This day's trek offers a cultural insight into the local lifestyle. Upon reaching Jakhol, relax and reflect on the incredible experiences of the trek.

Conclude your journey with a drive from Sankri back to Dehradun. Reflect on the memories and adventures of the past days as you return to the city. This marks the end of a remarkable trek through the Himalayas.
